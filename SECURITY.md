# Security Policy

We take the security of our projects seriously and appreciate all responsible disclosures.

---

## Supported Versions

We provide security updates for the **latest major version** of this project.  
Older versions may not receive security fixes unless explicitly stated.

---

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, **please do NOT file a public issue**.

Instead, report it privately to our security team:

**info@ciscod.com**

Please include:

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Relevant logs, screenshots, or proof-of-concept

We aim to acknowledge all reports within **72 hours**.

---

## Security Process

After receiving your report:

1. We investigate and validate the issue.
2. We assess the impact and affected versions.
3. We develop a fix, patch, or mitigation.
4. We coordinate a safe disclosure timeline with you.
5. We publish a release and document the fix.

We will keep you updated throughout the process.

---

## Disclosure Guidelines

We follow **responsible disclosure practices**:

- Do not publicly share the vulnerability until an official fix is released.
- Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is needed for investigation.
- Do not access user data or attempt data extraction.

We will credit your contribution in the release notes unless you prefer to remain anonymous.

---

## Thank You

We appreciate researchers, contributors, and community members who help keep our ecosystem secure.
